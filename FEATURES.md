# POLYMATH OS — Feature Brainstorm

---

## Visual Showstoppers
*Things that make someone stop and say "wait, what is that"*

**Thought Galaxy** — A live force-directed graph (D3.js) where every captured thought is a glowing node, colored by domain, connected by shared tags. Zoom out and you see your entire mind as a constellation. This alone is portfolio-defining.

**Ambient Focus Mode** — When the Pomodoro runs, the UI dims and a full-screen breathing particle field takes over. Subtle, beautiful, and it signals "I'm in flow" to anyone watching.

**Domain Radar Chart** — A spider/radar chart showing your 7-day balance across all 8 domains. Instantly shows if you've been neglecting Physics for a week. Looks incredible.

---

## Genuinely Game-Changing Utility

**Command Palette (`Cmd+K`)** — Like Linear or Vercel. One shortcut opens a fuzzy-search palette for every action: capture, filter, start timer, find a thought. Makes it feel like a real OS.

**Voice Capture** — Web Speech API. Hit a button, speak the thought, it transcribes + classifies automatically. Perfect for ADHD — zero friction, zero typing.

**AI Morning Brief** — First open of the day shows a Claude-generated card: *"3 open tasks from yesterday. Your Physics thread is 4 days cold. Today's suggested focus: Writing."* One API call, massive value.

**The Forge** — Select 2+ thoughts from different domains, hit "Forge" — Claude synthesizes a connection or emergent idea between them. *"Your Physics note on emergence + your AI/ML question about self-attention → possible insight about..."*

**Thought Aging** — Uncompleted tasks and ideas visually decay over time. After 3 days, a thought's opacity drops. After 7, it glows red. Forces you to act, archive, or delete. Very ADHD-friendly.

**Serendipity Engine** — Every 30 minutes, a random old insight surfaces quietly in the corner. Reconnects you with ideas you forgot you had.

---

## Stats That Feel Alive

**Focus Heatmap** — GitHub-style contribution grid, but for focus sessions. 90 days of your thinking history in one glance.

**Capture Velocity** — A sparkline graph showing thoughts per day over the last 2 weeks. See when you're most generative.

**Domain Time Clock** — A donut chart showing where your Pomodoro hours actually went this week.

---

## Wild / Unique

**Time Capsule** — Seal a thought to be revealed in 30/60/90 days. You write to your future self. It reappears with a notification when the date hits.

**Distill** — Select 5–10 thoughts from a domain, hit Distill — Claude compresses them into one sharp insight paragraph. Like a personal research summary.

**Thought Streaks** — Daily capture streak per domain, Duolingo-style. Miss 3 days in Physics and it tells you. Gamifies the habit.
