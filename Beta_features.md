CAPTURE & INPUT

  Neural Storm Mode
  Hold Space on the home screen. The entire UI collapses to a single massive textarea that takes over the screen — full
   black, pulsing border in your dominant domain color. You brain-dump thoughts at machine-gun speed. Every submission
  fires a particle that arcs toward the knowledge graph in the corner. No waiting for classification — everything      
  queues. After you stop (5s silence), the screen erupts: all queued thoughts get batch-classified, particles fly to   
  their domains, XP floods in. A "Storm Report" shows: X thoughts in Y seconds, domains activated, fastest streak.     

  Voice Capture
  Native Web Speech API, zero libraries. Hit V on home. The UI goes into listen mode — a raw oscilloscope waveform     
  animates in the background (canvas, drawing mic amplitude in real-time). Words appear as you speak, materializing    
  letter-by-letter. 2 seconds of silence auto-submits. Feels like thinking out loud into a machine that listens.       

  Quantum Superposition Thoughts
  For genuinely uncertain thoughts — tag them "superposition" on capture. The card renders as two overlapping
  semi-transparent versions alternating with a shimmer animation, domain label flickering between two possibilities.   
  Once you act on it (link to a boss, mark done, assign priority), it "collapses" with a starburst. Based on actual    
  physics metaphor — observations collapse superposition.

  VISUALIZATION

  Consciousness Timeline
  A full-page horizontal infinite-scroll timeline — your intellectual life as a river. Each domain is a colored stream 
  flowing left to right. Thoughts float above as bubbles. Focus sessions are shown as rapids (turbulent, animated      
  lines). Achievements are waterfalls. Zoom out to months, zoom in to a single day. Streams merge when you had
  cross-domain days. You can scrub through your own mind's history like a video. Pure canvas 2D, no libraries.

  Galaxy Domain Map
  Replace the domain table on the Profile page with a galaxy. Each domain is a star system — brightness = XP, size =   
  thought count, proximity to center = recency. Stars pulse when recently active, go dark/gray when neglected.
  Connecting lines between domains appear when you have cross-domain thoughts. The whole thing rotates slowly. Click a 
  star → zooms in, shows that domain's stats and last 5 thoughts. Built in canvas.

  Mood Topography
  Your energy log over time rendered as a 3D terrain — procedurally generated, no lib. High energy days = mountain     
  peaks, low energy = valleys. Flat-shaded triangles in a cool low-poly aesthetic. You can rotate it. See patterns:    
  maybe you always crash Sundays, peak Wednesdays. The terrain shifts in real-time as you add new energy entries.      

  Thought DNA Helix
  Your knowledge graph rendered as a double helix rotating in 3D (projection math only, canvas). Each strand is a      
  domain. Base pairs are cross-domain connections. Rotate it, zoom in, click a base pair to see the two thoughts it    
  connects. AI finds "mutations" — thoughts that connected two previously unconnected domains for the first time. Those
   show as glowing red base pairs.

  FOCUS & SESSIONS

  Singularity Focus Mode
  When a focus session starts, the entire app slowly implodes. UI elements spiral inward toward the timer over 3       
  seconds like matter falling into a black hole — CSS transforms, not removing elements. The timer becomes a pulsing   
  event horizon. Particles orbit it. To navigate away requires "escaping" — a visual drag-out-of-the-singularity       
  gesture. Makes focus feel like a real state you enter, not just a timer you start.

  Focus Depth Rings
  Around the focus timer, concentric rings that fill up as uninterrupted depth increases. First ring at 10 min, full at
   25. Second ring: 25-50 min. Third: 50-90 min. Rings rotate faster as depth increases. They persist visually between 
  sessions as a permanent record of deep work — the timer screen shows all rings you've ever completed as faint        
  history.

  Identity Mode Aesthetic Shift
  When you pick an identity mode (Scholar, Builder, Artist, etc.) the CSS custom properties of the entire app shift    
  dynamically. Scholar: cool blues, tighter spacing, slightly serif-feeling accents. Builder: terminal green, monospace
   everywhere, scanline effect on backgrounds. Artist: warmer palette, gradient-heavy, organic asymmetric accents. Not 
  just a color theme — actual personality shifts in the UI.

  AI & INTELLIGENCE

  The Oracle
  Full-screen mode. A massive 3D glowing orb (canvas — Fibonacci sphere like the AntiTimer but bigger, slower, more    
  dramatic, shifting colors). You ask it questions about your own mind: "What patterns do you see in my thinking?"     
  "What domain am I neglecting?" "What's my most connected idea?" It pulls your actual state — thought history, XP     
  spread, session patterns — and sends it to Claude with that context. The orb pulses while generating. The response   
  materializes letter by letter from the center of the orb. Feels like consulting an ancient intelligence that actually
   knows you.

  Thought Distillation
  Select multiple thoughts (checkbox mode). Hit "Distill." Animation: the selected cards visually fly toward each      
  other, merge into a glowing compressed sphere in the center of the screen, then shatter into one new card — the      
  AI-synthesized crystallized insight from all of them. The resulting card has a special "distilled" visual treatment: 
  heavier glow border, different background texture, diamond icon. Worth more XP. Tags all source thoughts as
  contributing.

  Serendipity Portal (Active Mode)
  Right now serendipity is ambient. Give it an active mode: a dedicated view that shows two random old thoughts side by
   side, asks "Are these connected?" Yes → draws an edge in the knowledge graph + XP reward. No → both get a relevance 
  bump for next time. Over time this builds a personally-trained connection layer. Visualize your yes/no history as a  
  growing web.

  GAME MECHANICS

  The Nemesis System
  Boss battles you abandon come back. A nemesis boss reappears after 7 days of inactivity, with mutated phases (phase  
  titles get darker/more intense, XP gates increase by 50%). Shows with a red skull on the boss card and a "YOUR       
  NEMESIS RETURNS" notification. Creates genuine dread and urgency. Third time it comes back — it's legendary tier,    
  huge XP on defeat.

  Chain Reaction Events
  Engineer overlaps: complete a quest during a boss battle while at 3x combo in a focus session → triggers CHAIN       
  REACTION. The screen briefly goes into overdrive — all elements spark with electricity (canvas overlay), XP
  multiplier flashes, a special achievement logs the exact chain. Rare by design. Makes you want to deliberately set up
   these overlaps.

  Resonance Detector
  The app monitors capture cadence in real-time. When you hit 3+ thoughts in a domain within 4 minutes, building on    
  each other — it detects "resonance" and the background of the capture area starts vibrating at that domain's color   
  frequency (subtle CSS pulse). An indicator glows: ◈ RESONANCE ACTIVE. This tells you: you're in a flow state right   
  now — don't stop. Logging a session during resonance = double XP.

  Cognitive Load Gauge
  Permanent ambient display — a small thermometer/gauge somewhere in the UI. Calculates load from: overdue
  high-priority thoughts + active boss phases + unresolved quests + days since last focus session. When it hits red,   
  the gauge pulses. Clicking it opens Chaos Mode directly. Gives ADHD brains a real-time "temperature" of how
  overwhelmed the system is — externalizes the feeling into data.

  WILD CARDS

  Mind Palace (3D Navigable Space)
  A first-person navigable 3D space — built entirely in canvas 2D with raycasting projection (Wolfenstein-style, no    
  WebGL). Each domain is a room. Walking through shows your thoughts as objects on shelves/walls. Room size = domain   
  XP. Room decoration density = thought count. You literally walk through your knowledge. Navigate with arrow keys.    
  Thoughts can be interacted with by walking up to them.

  Hypnagogic Screensaver
  After 8 minutes of idle, the app enters a dream state. The knowledge graph floats to full-screen, nodes slow to 10%  
  speed, colors shift toward purples and deep blues, old thoughts drift upward like embers from a fire and fade out.   
  Background: slow slow-moving particle field. Touches/clicks wake it back up with a snap. Makes the app feel alive on 
  a second monitor — it's not "off," it's dreaming.

  Polymath Death Clock (Memento Mori Mode)
  Toggle-on feature in settings. Shows a faint counter in the corner: estimated years of intellectual life remaining   
  based on average lifespan. Not morbid — motivating. Updates as you capture. Every 100 captures, a "you used your time
   well" flash. Every week without a session: the counter pulses red for a second. Forces you to confront that your    
  curiosity has an expiration date.
